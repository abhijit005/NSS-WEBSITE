ABOUT NSS

The National Service Scheme (NSS) is a government-led public service program launched in 1969 by the Ministry of Youth Affairs & Sports, Government of India. It aims to develop students' personality through community service while instilling values of social responsibility, leadership, and teamwork. Guided by the motto
"Not Me, But You,"

Why Join NSS

1️⃣

Serve the Community

Engage in activities that create a positive impact.

2️⃣

Enhance Leadership & Teamwork

Learn to lead, coordinate, and work with diverse groups.

3️⃣

Build social responsibility

Cultivate a sense of belonging and readiness to serve.

Our Vision

To build a socially responsible and self-reliant youth who actively contribute to nation-building, community service, and sustainable development while fostering values of unity, discipline, and leadership.

Our Mission

To empower students with leadership skills, teamwork, and social responsibility through various community-based initiatives, enhancing their overall development and fostering a culture of service.
